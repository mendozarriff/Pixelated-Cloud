About

Established in 2017, Pixelated Cloud is a web development company from San Diego California. Founder Jose Mendoza started his web development journey in the early 2000s. Even though Jose had great passion for technology, life took him to a different path. He was a lab technician in UC San Diego and became a scientist at IONIS Pharmaceuticals in Carlsbad California.

Mendoza never stopped his curiosity for technology, taking web and programming courses all throughout his science career. And in early 2017 Jose decided to leave science behind and devote his life to technology. And that is how PIXC was born.
